    Welcome to NDBC

    Notre Dame Business Club is the first business club at the college level in Bangladesh and the 4th club in Notre Dame College. Its goal is to provide practical business learning. Founded by Fr. Stephen Gomez (CSC) and former principal Fr. Benjamin Costa (CSC) in 1973, NDBC continues to introduce the wonderful world of business to its members and equip them to strategize complex business plans.
